Recommended Places to visit in Pakistan

Kumrat Valley

Kumrat Valley, is a valley in Upper Dir District, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa province, of Pakistan. It is one of the picturesque valleys of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, and a charming spot for tourists. In every summer season, thousands of tourists from different areas of the country visit to Kumrat Valley and enjoy the vegetation and chill weather.

Jahaz Banda

Let us introduce you to Jahaz Banda and Katora Lake, an epic hike in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa and one of Pakistan’s hidden gems. Jahaz Banda is a beautiful alpine meadow surrounded by massive snow-capped mountains. Think of a lush green pasture with towering peaks looming over it.

Naltar Valley

Naltar valley is located near Gilgit Baltistan, and it is a forested region well known for its magnificent mountain scenery. There are three very famous lakes in this area; Strangi Lake, Blue Lake, and Badlok Lake. “Halima garden” is a natural lavish green garden well known in this area.

Umbrella waterfall

The Umbrella Waterfall is one of the beautiful waterfalls of Abbottabad KPK Pakistan. This waterfall is located near the Sajikot waterfall near Poona Village. The Umbrella waterfall is very much famous because of its unique and attractive natural architecture. Besides, crystal clear water is running through the big rocks. The water showers create an amazing view which is heart freshening. The ambiance is awesome. One can feel how beautiful mother nature is.

Kachura lake

The Kachura Lake refers to the three lakes in skardu valley. These three lakes are Upper Kachura, Lower Kachura, and less known Zambakha Lake. Upper and lower Kachura Lake is at an altitude of 2,500 meters. The Upper Kachura Lake is mostly unexplored by tourists because of its rough terrain. The Lower Kachura Lake, popularly known as Shangrila Lake, is nature’s gem in the beautiful north of Pakistan. Zambakha Lake is in the tiny village of Zambakha next to Shangri-la.
